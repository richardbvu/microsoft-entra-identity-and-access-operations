Issue observed
Attempted to rename older mentor-era user accounts in Entra Admin Center.

Result
Core identity fields could not be edited from the cloud interface.

Finding
The affected accounts appear to be synced from on-premises Active Directory, so the "source of authority" is on-prem AD rather than Entra.

Conclusion
Cloud-side rename for those synced users is restricted. Changes should be made in on-prem AD and then synchronized to Entra.
