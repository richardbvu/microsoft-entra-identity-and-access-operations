Authentication-Strength-Method-Mismatch

- Carol Mercer has Software OATH / Google Authenticator registered
- Normal MFA policy works with Carol’s OATH code
- Created/tested CA policy requiring Phishing-resistant MFA
- Both normal MFA and phishing-resistant policies applied to Carol
- Carol could satisfy normal MFA but could not satisfy phishing-resistant MFA
- Root cause: registered method did not meet required authentication strength
- Returned policy to normal MFA / safe state
- Retested Carol sign-in successfully with Software OATH

Takeaway:
- Multiple CA policies can apply at the same time
- The user must satisfy the combined requirements
- Having MFA registered does not mean the user can satisfy every MFA-related CA policy
- Authentication strength controls the required quality/type of MFA
